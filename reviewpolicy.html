Review System

Hello! I am now accepting books and products/merchandise to review on my blog. And on that note, here are a few things you should know about if you want your book/s and products/merchandise to be reviewed by me.

FOR BOOKS:

Preferred Genres

  • adventure
  • contemporary*
  • -dystopian
  • -mystery/thriller/suspense*
  • -paranormal
  • -science fiction (sci-fi)*
  • -fantasy
  • -young adult*

Unwanted Genres

  • chic lit
  • erotica
  • non-fiction
  • (exclusively) romance

I prefer physical copies of books but some e-books will be entertained on a case-to-case basis. I also accept ARCs. Do remember that I am currently residing in the Philippines (for shipping purposes).

You can send me a request for review along with the following info:

  • title of book
  • book cover (pic)
  • author
  • genre
  • synopsis
  • publishing date (optional)
  • number of pages (optional)
  • series the book belongs to (if any)

Reminders:

  • I review all books fairly and give the proper verdict according to my honest reading experience. I assure you that whatever verdict I give is unbiased and truthful. (For more info about verdicts, visit my "Review System" page)
  • I do not guarantee a speedy reading. To fully give an honest and fair review, I do not rush my reading. I am not assuring a quick book review. But if you have a specific deadline for me to meet, we can talk things through.
  • I will not accept a book from a series if I haven't read its preceding book(s).
  • I promise not to give away or sell the copies I receive but I can host book giveaways as long as you tell me to.
  • I will only entertain requests sent to this email address: judereads(at)gmail(dot)com
  • I treat every "reviewee" with utmost respect, especially during conversations. But please remember that respect is a two-way street. :)
  • To know more about how I do book reviews, visit my book reviews page. My style is usually a "recommending style", meaning I write as if I am recommending it to a person who has never read the book. That is why my reviews are spoiler-free (unless otherwise stated). If you want me to add certain non-opinionated details to the book review, tell me in advance so that I could add it right away.

FOR PRODUCTS MERCHANDISE:

I repay my sponsors by posting their ad on my blog for some time, so it's basically a win-win system. I receive a product, and in return I'll post a review about it and I'll advertise you on my blog as well (sidebar and sponsors page). Check out my product reviews page if you'd like to read some of the product reviews I've done. You can email me at and we'll figure something out. :)

And that's it! Thanks for reading and I look forward to working with you! :)
